CORAKA SERVICES

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

The process of arranging, planning, and carrying out a project from beginning to end or a specific area of the project. It entails organizing people, tasks, and resources to accomplish particular objectives within a predetermined timeframe and financial constraints.

COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT

In the digital age, community management often takes place online, where individuals can connect and interact through various platforms such as social media, forums, online groups, and websites. A community manager facilitate and foster positive interactions within the community, acting as a bridge between the community members and the organization or brand.

MODERATIONS

24/7 chat moderation refers to the continuous monitoring and management of online chat platforms or messaging systems by trained moderators around the clock. The purpose of 24/7 chat moderation is to ensure a safe and respectful environment for users, prevent inappropriate or harmful content, and enforce community guidelines or terms of service.

CHATBOT EXPERT

A Chatbot expert plays a crucial role in developing, implementing and managing chatbot systems within a project community, aiming to enhance user experience, streamline communication, and provide valuable assistance to community members.

CONTENT CREATION

The process of creating useful and interesting content for a variety of platforms and media, including websites, blogs, social media, videos, podcasts, and more, is referred to as content creation. It entails producing unique, pertinent material that appeals to the target market and fulfills a certain need, such as promoting a good or service or providing entertainment or education.

PROMOTIONS

In the context of marketing and business, promotions refer to a variety of techniques and actions performed to promote a good, service, or brand in order to raise awareness of it, draw in customers, and improve sales. Promotions are essential for raising awareness, stoking interest, and inspiring action among target audiences.
